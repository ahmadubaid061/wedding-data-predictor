Privacy Policy for Wedding Predictor

Important Note: Wedding Predictor is a fun entertainment website. We collect minimal data for entertainment purposes only. All predictions are randomly generated and not based on actual personal data analysis.

We collect the following information when you use our website:

  • Friend's Name: The name you enter for wedding predictions (for entertainment purposes only)

  • Contact Information: Only if you voluntarily contact us through our contact form

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Generating fun wedding predictions
  • Displaying fun statistics on the website

3. Data Storage and Security

We store minimal data:

We use the following third-party services:

These services have their own privacy policies which we recommend you review.

We may use:

  • Local Storage: To store prediction count and statistics (non-personal)

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your browser's local storage at any time
  • Not enter any personal information

8. Entertainment Disclaimer

Important: Wedding Predictor is purely for entertainment purposes. The predictions are randomly generated and have no basis in reality. We do not actually predict wedding dates or analyze personal data for predictions.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated date will be displayed at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
